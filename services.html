Services

For over 40 years, Pactrol Controls Limited has designed and manufactured highly innovative electronic control systems for a wide range of gas and oil fired appliances in the domestic, commercial and industrial markets.

Pactrol Controls Limited is situated in the North West of England, in Ashton In Makerfield near Wigan, between Manchester and Liverpool.

The company's facilities include state-of-the-art computer aided design systems, staffed by experienced specialists.

Our extensive electronic assembly facilities, located in Eastern Europe, are set up to provide OEM customers with flexible and competitive electronic assemblies for installation in their own products.

The range of Pactrol standard products is also manufactured in Eastern Europe and stocks are held to meet customers call-off or regular ordering needs.
