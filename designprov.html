Pactrol Controls Limited Centre of Excellence is situated in Ashton In Makerfield. The company's facilities include state-of-the-art computer aided design systems, staffed by experienced specialists.

Our extensive electronic assembly facilities, located in Eastern Europe , are set up to provide OEM customers with flexible and competitive electronic assemblies for installation in their own products.

The range of Pactrol standard products is also manufactured on a production line basis and stocks are held to meet customers call-off or regular ordering needs.
