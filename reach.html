Reach Compliance


Pactrol Controls Ltd does not manufacture or import into the EU any substance in quantities of one tonne or more per year and does not produce or import articles containing a substance in quantities of more than one tonne per producer / importer per year which is intended to be released from the article under normal or reasonably foreseeable conditions of use.

Therefore, Pactrol Controls Ltd is not under the obligation to register substances under REACH. It is anticipated that Pactrol Controls Ltd suppliers effect all necessary registrations .
