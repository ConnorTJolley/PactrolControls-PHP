About Pactrol Controls Ltd


Product Automation and Control Limited, was formed in 1968 as a private company. In 1974 the name was changed to Pactrol Controls Limited and in 1984 another change, IMI Pactrol Ltd.

June 1998 saw the greatest change, where the company was acquired by White-Rodgers. White Rodgers is a Division of Emerson Climate Technologies.

Under the Emerson banner, manufacturing was moved to a campus in Slovakia and more recently to Hungary. The move to Hungary realises improved production facilities offering greater efficiency, higher output and increased flexibility. This will satisfy our customers changing requirements for years to come.

In 2004 a new centre of excellence in Lancashire was commissioned, offering Design, Development, Quality, Customer service and a 1200m2 modern warehouse. We can offer:-

  • Design and Development which would be customer focused
  • Application and Life testing
  • Customer support
  • Quality support
  • Sales support
  • Efficient deliveries, including Kan Ban and daily deliveries
  • Safety stock provision

    • Pactrol Controls Ltd are the sole distributers for all White-Rodgers products throughout Europe
